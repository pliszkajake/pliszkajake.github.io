Giving Haircuts

Chopping Away

I enjoy giving haircuts to my friends, family, and peers. While I am NOT a professional barber and do not advertise myself as such, I find joy in helping others look fresh with the skills I have acquired from internet tutorials in addition to an abundance of trial-and-error experience. I have improved significantly since starting in 2022, continuing to deliver whenever anyone needs help cleaning up their hairstyle.

Some examples of haircuts I have given others include...

Taper Fade (pictured below):
A fade in the temple area on both sides of the head as well as on the back neckline
Mid Fade:
A fade all around the head starting about halfway up the head
Burst Fade:
A fade from the temple area to behind the ear on both sides of the head
